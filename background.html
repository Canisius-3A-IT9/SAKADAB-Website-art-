Background and Significance of the Project

The Problem


The current challenges SAKADAB is facing are financial problems. SAKADAB is an institution which provides housing and livelihood for PWDs. However, their lack of funding makes them unable to fulfil all of their needs. According to their official website, their priority needs are a multi-purpose hall, a health center, a partition of the mess hall and kitchen, metal craft, carpentry tools & equipment, a covered walkway and roofing of the external kitchen.


The Intervention


The promotion of the handicrafts produced by our partner institution is a project that we suggest. Crafted consumer goods like individualized keychains and face masks are sold by SAKADAB. According to a document prepared by Nathaniel Limbo, the AdDU jhs community can assist SAKADAB by promoting the products they sell. Since a lot of people from our generation are inclined to social media, it would be a great idea to utilize the internet for this matter. Reposting content from their Facebook page and creating our own educational content to share on various social media platforms are good places to start. This idea can benefit the institution in the long run since the material we post on social media is not simply withdrawn, and because when people share the stuff we promote, we reach more people and educate them.

The project prioritizes the promotion of handicraft products SAKADAB offers. The main problem of our institution is their lack of funding. This causes them to have a hard time acquiring needed medication and necessary parts of their facility. SAKADAB has talked about their difficulties in buying medicine and their plans on renovating and adding more places in their facility. However, due to their financial problems, it is hard for them to achieve this. This project aims to promote the institution and their services and products to the public and encourage people to visit SAKADAB. Since their only source of income is through handicraft products, advertising or promoting this can greatly help them. If the project is a success, the institution can achieve financial stability and buy medications with ease. They can also provide more centers and renovate their facilities to promote their advocacy.

In addition to this, we highly encourage businesses and companies to partner up with SAKADAB to better promote their products. This is also to hopefully employ the beneficiaries and to stabilize their income.
